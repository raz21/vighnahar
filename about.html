Who We Are

We, at VIGHNAHAR ENTERPRISES dedicate to provide quality and efficient services to our customers as per International Standards. We are business leaders in providing export services to export houses and industries. We are Government approved fumigation and heat-treating agents for exports and our certificate is valid in all the countries. Our business activities are:

1) Importing packing grade pine wood and trading in the local market
2) Manufacture wooden cases, pallets & crates and supply to export houses
3) Supply of packaging products - plastic drums, straps, scratch films, pkg belts, etc.
4) Provide export packaging & lashing services of machine on rack and in containers.
5) Fumigation of Agricultural produce in import & export containers and warehouse
6) Provide heat treatment wooden products and certify as per international standard

What we do

VIGHNAHAR ENTERPRISES dedicated to manufacture world class industrial wooden packaging products as per ISPM-15 standards. Our company was established in 2013 by Miss. Pratima.R.Ghangale with his education in MBA and vast experience in wooden packaging Industry & fumigation Miss.Pratima is leading the company with an aim to provide professional and personalized services in packaging. It is then no wonder that for years, our clients have had a long association with our company Vighnahar Enterprises provide a wide range of packaging products such as, wooden pallets (different variety of pallets: ISO standards, two-way entry, four-way entry, and Euro pallets), wooden boxes (for Domestic and Export purposes), and wooden crates. Our products undergo chemical fumigation to ensure protection from physical or environmental factors. We specialize in customized heavy machinery packaging and are experts in wooden packaging products that are sent in overseas shipments to Europe, Asia and other continents.
